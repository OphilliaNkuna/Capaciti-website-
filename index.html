About Us

Established in 2010, CAPACITI, part of the UVU Africa Group, is at the forefront of preparing young individuals for the tech sector. CAPACITI has emerged as one of South Africaâ€™s leading digital talent accelerators, annually training and placing approximately 1000 unemployed youth. CAPACITI is dedicated to fostering the inclusive growth of the digital economy by creating a pipeline of future-fit, skilled individuals equipped with relevant digital skills aligned with industry demand. Since its inception, CAPACITI has been refining solutions that aim to economically integrate talent, by providing scalable digital skills training and preparing them for the evolving workplace of the future. As a non-profit organisation, CAPACITI leverages partner investments to deliver demand-driven, targeted technical training that positively impacts lives.

Our Core Values

Innovation

CAPACITI drives innovation by equipping young talent with cutting-edge digital skills and connecting them to industry opportunities. Through technology-driven training, partnerships, and scalable digital platforms, CAPACITI empowers individuals and organisations to thrive in the fast-changing digital economy

Integrity

Do the right thing, always. our unwavering commitment to always do the right thing. It is the foundation of our culture, built upon honesty in our communication, accountability for our actions, and respect for everyone we engage with.

Customer Focus

Our customer focus means we put our clients at the heart of every decision we make. We are dedicated to deeply understanding their unique challenges and goals, and we measure our own success by the value we create for them. We commit to being proactive, reliable partners who not only respond to needs but anticipate them, always going the extra mile to ensure their long-term success and build unshakable trust.

Our Mission & Vision

CAPACITI aims to democratize tech careers and foster inclusive societies in Africa by providing training, mentorship, and access to digital opportunities.
